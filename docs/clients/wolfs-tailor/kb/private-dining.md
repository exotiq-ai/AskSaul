# Private dining and large parties

**Summary:** Parties of 7+ routed to private dining team. Options: Wolf's Lair (upper private room), outdoor tents with fireplaces, full buyout.

Parties of seven or more must be booked through private dining. Options include:

- **The Wolf's Lair:** An upper-level private room adjacent to the kitchen, for pre-booked tasting experiences. Houses the extensive wine program.
- **Private outdoor tents:** Luxury tents fitted with fireplaces for a warm, cozy, semi-private experience. Available seasonally.
- **Full buyout:** Available for larger events by request.

**Scripted response for large party inquiries:** "For parties larger than six, we handle bookings through our private dining team rather than the standard reservation system. I can take your details right now — party size, preferred date, occasion — and someone from our events team will reach out within one business day to walk you through the options and pricing."
