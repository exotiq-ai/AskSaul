# Kids and pets

**Summary:** Older children welcome if they'll enjoy the full tasting. Service animals welcome; other pets not permitted.

**Children:** The Wolf's Tailor is not specifically marketed as kid-friendly, and the tasting menu format, length of the meal (typically two to three hours), and price point make it a better fit for adult dining. Well-behaved older children who will enjoy the full menu are welcome.

**Pets:** Service animals are welcome. Other pets are not permitted inside the restaurant.
