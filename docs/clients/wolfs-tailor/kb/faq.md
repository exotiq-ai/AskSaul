# Frequently asked questions — fast recall

**Summary:** Quick answers for hours, same-night booking, pricing, dress code, allergies, walk-ins, parking, kids, vegetarian, menu preview, a la carte, large groups, outdoor seating, cancellation, gift cards, dinner length, BYOB.

**Q: What are your hours?**
A: We're open Tuesday through Sunday, from five PM to eight thirty PM for last seating. We're closed on Mondays.

**Q: Can I get a reservation for tonight?**
A: Let me check Tock for you. Reservations for tonight depend on availability, and we also hold a portion of the dining room for walk-ins each evening.

**Q: How much is dinner?**
A: The indoor tasting menu is approximately two hundred fifty dollars per person. Beverage pairings are additional. A twenty two percent fair labor and wellness fee is added to the final bill.

**Q: Is there a dress code?**
A: No strict dress code. Come as you are — most guests are somewhere between smart casual and business casual.

**Q: Can you accommodate my gluten allergy?**
A: We do accommodate severe food allergies with advance notice, though there are a few we can't work around because of the current menu — specifically soy, allium, and citrus. If you'd like to share the details, I'll note them on your reservation.

**Q: Can I just walk in?**
A: Yes, we save a portion of the dining room for walk-ins each evening. The backyard garden is also first-come, first-served in warmer months.

**Q: Where do I park?**
A: Street parking on Tejon and the surrounding blocks. It's usually easy to find a spot within a short walk of the front door.

**Q: Is it kid friendly?**
A: We welcome older children who will enjoy the full tasting experience, but the format, length, and price point make it a better fit for adult dining.

**Q: Do you have vegetarian options?**
A: Yes, with advance notice. Please let me note that on your reservation.

**Q: Can I see the menu?**
A: The menu changes seasonally and is presented at the table. We keep the specific courses a surprise, but I can tell you the current season's theme if that's helpful.

**Q: Do you offer a bar menu or small plates?**
A: We only offer the multi-course tasting menu. There's no a la carte option.

**Q: Can I book for ten people?**
A: Parties larger than six are handled through our private dining team. I can take your details and have someone reach out within a business day.

**Q: Is there outdoor seating?**
A: Yes, we have a garden and private outdoor tents with fireplaces. Tent reservations can be booked in advance, and the garden is first come first served.

**Q: How do I cancel?**
A: Cancellations are handled through Tock. Full refund with seventy two hours notice, fifty percent with forty eight hours, and unfortunately no refund within twenty four hours.

**Q: Do you do gift cards?**
A: Yes, gift cards are available through our Tock page.

**Q: How long does dinner take?**
A: Plan for two to three hours to enjoy the full experience.

**Q: Is it BYOB?**
A: No, we have a full beverage program with wine, cocktail, and zero-proof pairings.
