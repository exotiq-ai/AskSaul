# Payment

**Summary:** Tock prepay at booking. Amex, Discover, Mastercard, Visa accepted in-house. 22% fair labor & wellness fee added to in-house bill (NOT tip).

Accepted payment methods at the restaurant: American Express, Discover, Mastercard, and Visa. The reservation itself is prepaid through Tock using a credit card at the time of booking.

A twenty two percent fair labor and wellness fee is automatically added to the in-house bill. This is not a tip. Any additional gratuity added on top goes entirely to the staff.
