# Reservations

**Summary:** Booked via Tock (exploretock.com/wolfstailor), prepaid. Parties of 2–6 online, 7+ through private dining. Open first of month for next month. 72hr / 48hr / 24hr refund tiers.

**Primary booking channel:** Tock at exploretock.com/wolfstailor
**Reservations release:** Monthly on the first of the month, for the following month. For example, August reservations open July first.
**Party size:** Online reservations are available for parties of two to six. Parties larger than six must be handled through private dining.
**Walk-ins:** A portion of the dining room is saved for walk-ins each evening. The backyard garden is first-come, first-served during warmer months.

**Prepayment:** Reservations through Tock are prepaid at the time of booking. The full cost of the meal for all guests is charged up front. Beverage pairings and additional gratuity are charged at the table.

**Cancellation policy:**
- Seventy two hours or more in advance: Full refund
- Forty eight to seventy two hours in advance: Fifty percent refund
- Less than twenty four hours in advance: No refund

**If a guest wants to book:** Direct them to Tock at exploretock.com/wolfstailor. Explain the prepayment model clearly so there are no surprises. Offer to capture their allergy and occasion notes to pass along to the team.

**If a guest wants to cancel or change:** Cancellations must be handled through Tock. Explain the refund window. For emergency situations, offer to flag the guest's message to the management team for follow-up.

**If a guest asks about Capital One Dining:** The Wolf's Tailor has a Capital One Dining partnership. Eligible Capital One cardholders can access prime-time tables through the Capital One Dining platform.
