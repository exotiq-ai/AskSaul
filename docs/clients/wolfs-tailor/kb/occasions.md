# Occasions and special requests

**Summary:** Anniversaries, birthdays, proposals, gift cards, photography — team notes the occasion and adds personalized touches. Outside desserts generally not permitted.

The Wolf's Tailor is a popular destination for special occasions. Common requests:

- **Anniversaries and birthdays:** Note the occasion on the reservation. The team may offer a personalized touch like a printed menu with a message. Cakes and outside desserts are generally not permitted because of the tasting menu format.
- **Proposals and engagements:** The team is experienced with these. Flag it with as much advance notice as possible. Private tents are an excellent setting for this.
- **Gift cards:** Available through Tock.
- **Photography:** Respectful photography for personal use is welcome. No flash, no professional setups without prior arrangement.
