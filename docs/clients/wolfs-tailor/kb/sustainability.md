# Sustainability and sourcing

**Summary:** ~95% Colorado-sourced. House-grown White Sonora wheat. MASA Seed Foundation partnership. Zero-waste program. Part of Id Est Hospitality (Brutø, Dry Storage).

This matters to many guests and is a point of pride for the restaurant. Key facts:

- Approximately ninety five percent of ingredients are sourced from Colorado farms and purveyors
- The restaurant grows its own White Sonora wheat, a heritage grain, and sells the flour
- Seeds are sourced from the MASA Seed Foundation in Boulder
- Zero-waste ethos across kitchen and bar, including a wine cork recycling program
- Fermentation and preservation programs are central to the menu
- The Wolf's Tailor is part of the Id Est Hospitality family, which also operates Brutø and Dry Storage, the group's own milling operation
